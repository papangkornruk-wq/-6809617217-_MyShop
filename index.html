About us

       At Pekapar, we believe every piece of clothing deserves a second chance. We collect beautiful secondhand clothes in excellent condition, meticulously repair any flaws, and ensure they are perfectly clean before passing them on to you.

Why should you support us?

       When you choose a secondhand garment, you're doing more than just finding a unique piece; you're actively fighting against textile waste. The fast fashion industry's constant cycle of production creates immense pollution and fills landfills with discarded clothes. By giving a pre-loved item a second life, you're directly reducing demand for new production and helping to build a more sustainable world, one stylish outfit at a time.
